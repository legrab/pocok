## Objective

Create an independently packageable readiness lifecycle with explicit startup, failure, cancellation, shutdown, and restart behavior.

## Starting state

- The standalone repository contained only `Pocok.Primitives`.
- Reference code equated completion of `StartAsync` with readiness and left waiters hanging on failure, cancellation, and stop-before-ready.

## Decisions and deviations

- Use an instance-based readiness source instead of inheriting a specific hosting framework base class.
- Use opaque startup-cycle tokens to reject stale completion races.
- Keep caller cancellation separate from lifecycle cancellation and structured failure.
- Keep timeout policy outside shared readiness state.
