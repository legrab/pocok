# Third-Party Notices

No third-party runtime dependency or redistributed asset has been added yet.

Each future dependency entry must record:

| Field | Required information |
|---|---|
| Component | Package, SDK, tool, action, fixture, or asset name. |
| Version | Exact version or reviewed range. |
| Purpose | Why the dependency is needed and why the framework is insufficient. |
| License | SPDX identifier or reviewed license name and link. |
| Distribution | Whether it appears in source, binary packages, symbols, samples, or tooling only. |
| Obligations | Attribution, notice, source, patent, trademark, or redistribution requirements. |
| Review | Reviewer and date. |

Dependencies used only by CI or tests are still recorded when their license or generated output affects distribution. Vendor SDKs and standards-derived implementations require their own review and must not enter foundational packages indirectly.
